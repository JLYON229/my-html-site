📘 Legal Inquiries

Use these responses to remain compliant when clients ask legal questions:

❓ “Will I go to jail?”
🚫 I can't give legal advice, but that’s not typical in these cases.

❓ “Can they garnish my wages?”
🚫 Please speak with your creditor or consult a licensed attorney.

❓ “Do I have to go to court?”
🚫 Some clients do attend hearings while we continue working on the account.

❓ “Should I stop paying?”
🚫 Most clients find it helpful when payments are voluntarily past due.

❓ “Can I block creditor calls?”
🚫 Many use RWC (Request for Written Communication) under FDCPA.

❓ “Will you guarantee a settlement?”
🚫 We cannot promise one, but most legal accounts are resolved over time.
